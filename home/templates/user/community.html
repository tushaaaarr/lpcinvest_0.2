LPC in the Community

At LPC Invest we believe that supporting our local community is essential, we work to give back to the communities that helped raise/nurture our values and mission. We are forever inspired by the drive within our community and colleagues. And so, we want to share some of the ways in which we have been supporting our partners.

Supporting (The Dreams/The Future Of) Young People We have recently sponsored Eccles Rugby Clubs junior division, supporting the future stars of the sporting world. Home grown since 1897, Eccles RFC are a ambitious, progressive, inclusive community club. Who pride themsleves on offering Rugby for all. Creating an avenue through which the sport can be accessed is something the LPC Invest team believes in and something Eccles RFC upholds. LPC Invest share the ambition and passion of Eccles RFC and will continue to support their growth in the years to come. Through our partnership LPC Invest helps to maintain the facilities, keep the gates open and keep the pitches cut. See their amazing work for yourself - https://eccles.rfu.club/sponsors
