Places to Invest in Manchester

5 Places to invest in property during the Cost of Living Crisis

Manchester is a bustling city that's known for its thriving property market. Whether you're a seasoned investor or looking to get into the property market for the first time, Manchester offers plenty of opportunities to make a sound investment. This blog takes a closer look at some of the best areas to buy a property investment in Manchester, with facts and figures for rationale.

Ancoats
Ancoats is a trendy, up-and-coming area that's popular with young professionals and students. It's situated just east of Manchester city centre and has experienced significant regeneration in recent years. The cherry on the cake is that property prices in Ancoats have increased by over 30% in the last five years, making it an attractive area for investors looking for capital growth.

Salford Quays
Salford Quays is a flourishing waterfront area that's home to a range of businesses and attractions, including the BBC and The Lowry Arts Centre. Property prices in Salford Quays have increased by over 20% in the last five years, and rental yields are high due to the area's popularity with young professionals.

Deansgate
Deansgate is one of Manchester's busiest shopping and nightlife areas, with plenty of restaurants, bars, and clubs. The popularity of this area reflects in the property prices which have skyrocketed more than in the last five years, making Deansgate an investors-favorite spot for capital growth. The area is also favourable for short-term lets, which can be a profitable investment strategy.
Deansgate Tower Viadux
Standing proudly above Castlefield Locks and strategically connected to the iconic former railway station, Manchester Central, Viadux/ this development brings a unique opportunity to those wishing to live within the heart of the city centre. With Deansgate tram station so close residents will have easy access to other parts of the city such as Manchester Airport and Salford Quays. Manchester hotspots are just at the doorstep: galleries, museums, shopping centre such as Arndale Gallery, cafe's, bars and restaurants are just at the doorstep from this location.

Trafford
Trafford is a borough in Manchester that has become increasingly popular with property investors in recent years. Situated to the southwest of Manchester city centre, Trafford offers a range of attractive neighbourhoods and has experienced significant regeneration in recent years. One of the main reasons why Trafford is a great place for property investment is its strong house price growth. According to the latest data from Zoopla, property prices in Trafford have jumpedby over 7% in the last year, and by over 22% in the last five years. This indicates that Trafford is a high-demand area where property values are likely to continue to rise. Trafford also offers high rental yields for investors. According to our data at LPC, the average rental yield in Trafford is around 6%, which is significantly higher than the national average. This is due to the borough's strong demand from both young professionals and families, and its proximity to Manchester city centre.
Trafford’s Regeneration and Development
Trafford has also experienced significant regeneration and development in recent years, with a number of major projects underway. The £1 billion Manchester Airport City development, which is set to create thousands of new jobs, is situated in the borough, while the new £350 million Trafford Park Metrolink extension will improve transport links to the area. This development and regeneration make it an attractive location for businesses and individuals, which will likely drive demand for property investment.
Great Schools and Amenities
Trafford also offers great schools and amenities, which make it an attractive location for families. Trafford is home to a number of outstanding primary and secondary schools, as well as some of the best independent schools in the country. The borough also offers plenty of leisure facilities, including the Trafford Centre shopping mall, Manchester United Football Club's Old Trafford stadium, and a range of parks and green spaces. Trafford is a lucrative choice for property investment, offering strong house price growth, high rental yields, regeneration and development, and great schools and amenities. With its strong fundamentals and attractive prospects for future growth, Trafford is a promising location for property investors in the Greater Manchester area. Property prices in Didsbury have risen by over 15% in the last five years, and rental yields are high due to the area's popularity with families and young professionals.
