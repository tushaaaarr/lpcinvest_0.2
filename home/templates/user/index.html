Welcome to LPC Invest!

LPC Invest is an innovative Property Investment company based in Manchester, presenting an exceptional portfolio of exclusive and recently completed properties across the North-West, UK.

We specialise in serving homebuyers, first time property investors and portfolio extenders looking for the perfect and most profitable property. LPC Invest’s team has a collective 62 years of market wide experience to assist you in finding the ideal investment. We have a deep knowledge of the market and its current trends.

Our experienced, dynamic and progressive team will support you in reaching your property investment goals. LPC Invest hold access to the UK's most lucrative and exciting property investment opportunities, all delivered by established and award-winning developers. Our responsibility is to ultimately relieve investors' decision-making anxiety and assist them from beginning to end, easing property investment complexities.
