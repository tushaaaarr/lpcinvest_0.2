About Us

Who we are?

LPC Invest was founded in 2018 by a team of experienced property experts. We specialize in brand-new properties, off-plan developments, and completed projects in emerging neighbourhoods. Our goal is to provide our clients with the best possible service and help them find their dream property. LPC Invest was created to serve homebuyers, first-time property investors and portfolio extenders looking for the perfect and most profitable property. The LPC Invest team is a group of experienced professionals who have a deep knowledge of the property market. We are here to help you find that ideal investment and to build a lucrative portfolio.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to invest in property, whether they're looking for their forever home or searching for a profitable investment. We offer a wide range of services to help our clients achieve their goals, and we're always happy to answer any questions you may have. If you're thinking about investing in property, we urge you to take advantage of everything we have to offer at LPC Invest. We'll help you every step of the way, from finding the right property to ensuring it's a profitable investment. Contact us today to learn more!

The Beginning

LPC Invest was founded by Lee Chettoe and Louise Wareing in 2018. They had a shared belief that there was a better way to source property investment opportunities for their clients, one that was more bespoke and considered. LPC Invest strives to provide the highest quality opportunities for our clients by being approachable and innovative. We work with some of the UK's leading developers to bring you unique investment opportunities that you won't find anywhere else.

Our values are important to us here at LPC Invest - we are approachable, innovative, and committed to providing the highest quality opportunities for our clients. We want to make sure that you feel comfortable and confident when making an investment with us, which is why we will always take the time to get to know you and your needs before recommending any property options.

LPC invest Today

LPC Invest is a Manchester-based investment company specializing in property development. We work with clients, investors, and developers to create successful investment portfolios that offer the best returns possible. Our team of experts has a wealth of experience in the property market, and we use this knowledge to help our clients make informed decisions about their investments.

Whether you're looking to invest in buy-to-let property, or you're a developer looking for funding for your latest project, we can help you achieve your goals. Contact us today to find out more about how we can help you make the most of your investment.
